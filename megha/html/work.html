More about my work and interests
By now you already have an overview of what I do. Hence, I would be elaborating on some of the things I have already mentioned. Since early, I have been intrigued by the world of mathematics which pushed me further to read more and solve problems. I believe mathematics is an art which beautifully blends in our lives yet preserving it's overwhelming abstractness. I also like to take part in competitive coding.

I have been selected for the IMO training camp (India) two times, appeared for APMO twice and hold merit certificate in INMO. Besides these, I am also a National Awardee, an MTRP,KVPY,NTSE scholar and STSE topper. I have also been selected and awarded in numerous maths, science, informatics, language and astronomy olympiads and projects. However, I see all my accolades as motivation to try my best and stay as enthusiastic about my work as always, with the goal to render constructive contribution to my society someday.

I also believe in sustainable development and have been part of environment preservation and protection campaigns. I also love to spread knowledge as a result of which I absolutely love teaching. I love singing and listening to music in my leisure time. Furthermore, I sketch and paint and love delivering messages through them.
